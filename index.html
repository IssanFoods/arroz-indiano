About Arroz Indiano

At Arroz Indiano, we bring together the rich traditions of Indian cuisine with premium ingredients and authentic recipes. Every biryani is slow-cooked with fragrant basmati rice, handpicked spices and perfectly marinated meat, while every shawarma is freshly prepared with juicy grilled chicken and our signature sauces.


Our mission is simple — to deliver restaurant-quality food with exceptional taste, hygienic preparation and fast service. Whether you're ordering for yourself, your family or a celebration, Arroz Indiano promises an unforgettable dining experience.
